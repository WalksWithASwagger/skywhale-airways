# Skywhale Airways — Duty-Free · Round 4

Round 4 responds to KK's merch notes: center the **Skywhale Chest Print** art,
make patches and stickers feel like Skywhale Airways artifacts, keep the cute
baggage tag / pins / sticker-sheet energy, and retire the previous character
nickname. The blonde character is the **Time Traveller**.

Same aesthetic lock as earlier rounds: 1960s-70s airline-brochure surrealism,
hand-painted gouache, delicate ink outlines, pastel sky blue, dusty rose,
golden yellow, sage, warm paper grain, dreamy lost-airline decals. Avoid clean
corporate vector, modern tech polish, neon, glossy plastic, and generic stock
merch.

The current passport stamp is a cool idea but not the right execution. Keep the
concept parked for a future rework; do not feature the Round 2 stamp in the
public catalog.

## 01 — Skywhale chest patch

**For:** Embroidered patch, 3-4 in, jacket / tote / camera bag.

**Prompt:**
> Embroidered patch version of the Skywhale Airways chest print: a friendly
> golden skywhale banking across a pale-blue stitched field, tiny escort
> fish-aircraft below, a small retro airplane tracing a dotted route arc, and
> warm rounded lettering reading "SKYWHALE AIRWAYS". Include tiny bottom text
> "EST. NOWHEN". Merrowed border, satin and chain-stitch texture, 1970s airline
> souvenir patch, soft cream felt ground, pastel dusty rose, sage, gold, sky
> blue. Cute and collectible, not corporate.
