# Skywhale Airways — Duty-Free · Round 3

Same workflow + aesthetic lock as R1/R2 (rafiki / Gemini 3 Pro Image, `style:none`,
keyframes as texture/character reference, 1970s airline-brochure gouache). This
round leans into **the Time Traveller** (the blonde traveler) as the iconic character,
plus more lyric taglines and the first **embroidered patch**. The original Round
3 asset slugs were renamed on June 2, 2026 to remove the retired nickname from
file paths.

1. **01-one-bag-one-body** — "ONE BAG, ONE BODY" lyric + the Time Traveller with a
   single suitcase and a golden fish.
2. **02-time-traveller-floating** — die-cut, the Time Traveller weightless and
   horizontal, "GRAVITY STOPS INSISTING".
3. **03-time-traveller-walking** — die-cut, the Time Traveller walking with one
   suitcase, a fish above, pastel horizon.
4. **04-time-traveller-emblem** — circular portrait emblem, "THE TIME TRAVELLER
   · SKYWHALE AIRWAYS".
5. **05-clock-refuses-time** — "THE CLOCK REFUSES ORDINARY TIME" lyric + scrambled
   clock.
6. **06-runway-like-a-memory** — "THE RUNWAY OPENS LIKE A MEMORY" lyric + skywhale
   over a dotted runway.
7. **07-skywhale-patch** — embroidered-style patch (satin/chain stitch, merrowed
   border) of the skywhale + "SKYWHALE AIRWAYS".
