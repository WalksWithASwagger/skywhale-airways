# Skywhale Airways — Duty-Free · Round 3

Same workflow + aesthetic lock as R1/R2 (rafiki / Gemini 3 Pro Image, `style:none`,
keyframes as texture/character reference, 1970s airline-brochure gouache). This
round leans into **the long girl** (the blonde traveler) as the iconic character,
plus more lyric taglines and the first **embroidered patch**.

1. **01-one-bag-one-body** — "ONE BAG, ONE BODY" lyric + the long girl with a
   single suitcase and a golden fish.
2. **02-long-girl-floating** — die-cut, the long girl weightless and horizontal,
   "GRAVITY STOPS INSISTING".
3. **03-long-girl-walking** — die-cut, the long girl walking with one suitcase, a
   fish above, pastel horizon.
4. **04-long-girl-emblem** — circular portrait emblem, "THE LONG GIRL · SKYWHALE
   AIRWAYS".
5. **05-clock-refuses-time** — "THE CLOCK REFUSES ORDINARY TIME" lyric + scrambled
   clock.
6. **06-runway-like-a-memory** — "THE RUNWAY OPENS LIKE A MEMORY" lyric + skywhale
   over a dotted runway.
7. **07-skywhale-patch** — embroidered-style patch (satin/chain stitch, merrowed
   border) of the skywhale + "SKYWHALE AIRWAYS".
